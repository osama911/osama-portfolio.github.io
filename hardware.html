remote control car

During my training period a client requested a product that does the following:

A house bell with four buttons on the external panel

The first button makes a low bell sound

The second button makes a medium bell sound

The third button makes a loud house bell sound

The fourth button makes a light that flashes 5 times and then lights up for 5 minutes

The product could have been implemented with programmable controllers but after calculating the cost it was cheaper to make hardware

I designed a PCB for the internal and external panels and combined them together to reduce the cost of manufacturing and importing.
